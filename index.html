How to Use This Govt Form Image Tool

This website helps candidates quickly prepare images for online government forms in India. Start by uploading a JPG or PNG image of your passport photo or signature. Enter your target file size in kilobytes, such as 20KB for signature or 50KB for photo, and click Compress Image. The tool runs directly in your browser and shows a side-by-side before and after comparison so you can verify quality and size.

You can also optionally set width and height if a specific exam portal asks for exact dimensions. This is useful for SSC form image size rules, UPSC profile uploads, PAN card image uploads, and other recruitment forms where strict image limits are required. Because processing is client-side, your file is not sent to a server, helping with speed and privacy while applying for forms on mobile or desktop.

Common Photo & Signature Requirements (India)

  • SSC / Govt Exams: Photo: 20KB - 50KB, Signature: 10KB - 20KB (may vary by notification)
  • UPSC Forms: Photo: usually 20KB - 300KB, Signature: 20KB - 50KB
  • Banking (IBPS): Photo: 20KB - 50KB, Signature: 10KB - 20KB
  • Railway (RRB): Photo: 20KB - 50KB, Signature: 10KB - 40KB
  • PAN Card: Photo: 10KB - 200KB
  • Passport Size Photo India: 35mm x 45mm, typically 20KB - 100KB
  • Aadhaar Update: Photo: up to 100KB

Note: Requirements may change depending on the form. Always check the official notification. Common search terms include SSC photo size, signature size KB, and passport photo size India.
